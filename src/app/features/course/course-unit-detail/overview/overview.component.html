Course Details

The Diploma in Business Management offered by Westford University College is a level 8 program, comprising three modules worth 20 credit points each, totaling 60 credit points. The program, completed in three months, prepares learners for a bachelor's degree with potential module exemptions. It covers key management, operation, human resources, IT, and marketing concepts, providing a thorough understanding of organizational operations and their impact on growth.
This diploma addresses industry requirements, equipping learners with in-demand skills and knowledge of management roles, marketing strategies, and operations management. Delivered 100% online in a synchronous format, it allows students to connect with faculty and study from anywhere. Upon completion, learners receive a diploma awarded by Westford University College.
