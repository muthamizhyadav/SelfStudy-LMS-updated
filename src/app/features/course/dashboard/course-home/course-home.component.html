Diploma in Digital Marketing at SCQF level 8

Duration: 3 Months

Credits: 45

Modules:: 2

Level: SCQF Level 8

With the advent of technology and the age of digitalisation, it has become essential for professionals at both, entry as well as expert levels to be equipped with the knowledge and skills of digital marketing. Which include, but are not limited to, Social Media Marketing, Google Advertisements and Analytics and Search Engine Optimisation.
